Top Choices In Web Hosting



  • By: Amal Prakash
  • 11 February 2021



In today’s Society, if you have got something to say, do, sell or whatever it is, you are going to need a website and you are going to need a really good provider to make sure that your traffic is managed that people can see it’s visibility and that the website is well protected and so many things that make sure your website lives up to the standard of what your customers should expect from you. So, like all things Web Hosting services comes in all shapes and sizes in whether they are offering shared hosting, dedicated hosting, VPS, Manage Hosting, Server Cores, Different RAMs, SSL Certificates etc. ... its endless, there is just an endless number of things that web hosting servers offers to you because what everybody needs is different.

The best options out there for you for a number of reasons based on quality, budget, speed and performance and many more are some of the followings...

Bluehost

Bluehost is one of the most popular and one of the best choices for web hosting. They have been around for a long time. Bluehost have got great hosting at a great price and have a great reputation for supporting their customers. They have a 24/7 support on call or live chat. This is the one exception that makes Bluehost one of the best because most of the services have live chat only available. They also offer fantastic analytics in SEO tools and makes sure you get visibility for your site to optimize to make sure it performs and attracts a lot of visitors. And the Bluehost SEO tools scores and tracks specific keywords so that it would give you an idea of how you should optimize your site for the best search rankings. They also offer hosting that will support whatever direction your business goes. Once your websites gets the reach, Bluehost also provides easy ways for speeding up your website and improving the security. Bluehost covers everything you could possibly need and it's an overall top choice for beginners. If you are new to creating websites, Bluehost is really great because they offer a lot of ways for you to feel comfortable doing what you are doing.

Hostinger

Some of us out there are a little bit budget conscious, So the next top choice is Hostinger .Hostinger is one of the most affordable web host out there. Their lowest plans start at less than a dollar. So, for just cheapest rate you can go up and start using Hostinger and all of these rates they have are promotional So they will go up at renewal but you are able to get it for low cost if you do a longer subscription. Might sound risky but it's really a great value. Even after the promotional pricing it's still super low, the single web hosting plan comes at one of the lowest prices in the industry and the best part is that hostinger doesn’t feel like a cheap hosting. For the marketing to the user interface for managing the site, you feel like you are dealing with something that is a little more high-end. The Uptime, the site speed, the support are all good and they have a 99.9% Uptime guarantee along with 24/7 support whenever you need help. So, don’t expect them to be quite as good as the other host but at such a value its unbeatable.

SiteGround

We all know how keeping up with this world requires speed and quality performance. So, the last top choice will be SiteGround. They have free SSL, Email, CDN Backups, 30 days money back guarantee. One thing SiteGround comes with that lot of web hosts don’t - a Rabid fanbase and glowing reviews. 93% of the people that use SiteGround rated as excellent, and their reputation is stellar. Along with Bluehost, SiteGround and Bluehost represents two of Wordpress’s top 3 recommended web hosts. So Wordpress hosting is built into all of its shared hosting plans. You will get automatic updates, streamlined security and expert technical support .SiteGround’s infrastructure is fully optimized for Wordpress . Siteground’s best option for wordpress users. And where they really shine is their shared web hosting. SiteGround’s servers are ultra-fast and ultra-secure and they are constantly deploying new updates to keep them that way. SiteGround is also one of the top picks for cloud hosting. If you feel like a need to upgrade to more serious hosting power once you start generating all that super-duper traffic, SiteGround is a great option which makes upgrading really easy later. And the customer support is available across all channel – phone, chat, email and they really know what they are doing. SiteGround also have the most transparent Uptimes, its 99.9% annual uptime guarantee or you get a month of free hosting and they are always keeping you updated as to what it is to make sure that they are not cheating on you that.

So, there you have it, when coming to the best hosting platforms, these webhosts will have you covered in many bases.
